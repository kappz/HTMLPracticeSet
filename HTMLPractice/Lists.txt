    <h1>Scrambled Eggs</h1>
    <p> Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.</p>
    <h2>Ingredients</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>2 eggs</li>
      <li>itbs butter</li>
      <li>2tbs cream<li>
    <ul>
    <h2>Method</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>Melt butter in frying pan over a medium heat</li>
      <li>Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl</li>
      <li>Once butter has melted add cream and eggs</li>
      <li>Using a spatula fold the egges from the edge of the pant to center every 20 seconds</li>
      <li>When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat</li>
    </ol>
