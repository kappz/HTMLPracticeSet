Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • itbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the egges from the edge of the pant to center every 20 seconds
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat
